Daphne - Nymph of Magix

Daphne is the Nymph and crowned princess of Domino, and one of the nine nymphs of Magix (others being Politea, Omnia, Egerya, Tetis, Diane, Marlene, and Janice). She wears a signature masqarade mask, a headress which resembles angel wings,, a crown with a red jewel in it's tall spire, and long flowing arm ribbons. She also has two pigtails which hang down over her face.
